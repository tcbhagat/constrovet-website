Why Maharashtra CAs should test

Pain points this workflow targets

  • Manual tracking of MAHAGST, Professional Tax, MCA, and Labour updates.
  • Client transaction review happening after payment instead of before payment.
  • Difficulty proving which PDF, circular, or rule caused a block.
  • Repeated advisory work without a reusable decision trail.
  • MCA/source access failures creating evidence gaps that need explicit status tracking.
  • Need for CA-reviewed decisions before SME teams rely on compliance outputs.
