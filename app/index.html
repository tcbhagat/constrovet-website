Budget No new paid infrastructure

Allowed: GitHub Pages, Workspace Drive, Colab free runtime, and approved Gemini access.

Blocked without approval: Cloud Run, Cloud SQL, GCS, Firestore, paid VMs, paid Colab Pro, paid vector databases, and non-Gemini model APIs.

Evidence rules

What The Notebook Must Prove

The output remains decision-support only and must stay tied to retrieved document evidence.

Every finding needs a citation

Each finding must include file, page or sheet, and quoted span. Missing evidence goes into the honesty check.

Overruns are calculated only when supported

If Budget and Actual are both available, overrun is calculated as Actual - Budget. Positive overruns are separate leakage findings.

Baseline is not leakage

Contract value, BOQ value, planned spend, cumulative work done, and advance recovery remain baseline budget signals.

ESG stays separate

Carbon, energy, water, fuel, diesel, electricity, waste, and emissions are ESG metrics unless separate financial evidence is cited.
