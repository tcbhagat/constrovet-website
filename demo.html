7-Day Actions

  • Validate the 8-document evidence pack and confirm which records are missing.
  • Separate baseline budget values from leakage and overrun candidates.
  • Assign owners for procurement delay, material variance, idle resource, and ESG record gaps.

30-Day Actions

  • Close evidence gaps for Budget, Actual, delay cause, and cost impact.
  • Review recoverable leakage exposure with project, finance, contract, and procurement teams.
  • Create a monitoring view for schedule-to-cost impact and ESG documentation.

90-Day Actions

  • Convert recurring leakage patterns into procurement and cost-control checkpoints.
  • Set executive reporting rules for cited findings, missing evidence, and action owners.
  • Use pilot lessons to decide whether to expand Constrovet review across more projects.

What a Real Pilot Needs

A real pilot requires authorized records, processing permission, and a named review owner. If evidence is missing, Constrovet should show the gap instead of guessing.
