7-day actions

  • Validate the 8-document evidence pack and confirm which records are missing.
  • Separate baseline budget values from leakage and overrun candidates.
  • Assign owners for procurement delay, material variance, idle resource, and ESG record gaps.

30-day actions

  • Close evidence gaps for Budget, Actual, delay cause, and cost impact.
  • Review recoverable leakage exposure with project, finance, contract, and procurement teams.
  • Create a monitoring view for schedule-to-cost impact and ESG documentation.

90-day actions

  • Convert recurring leakage patterns into procurement and cost-control checkpoints.
  • Set executive reporting rules for cited findings, missing evidence, and action owners.
  • Use pilot lessons to decide whether to expand Constrovet review across more projects.

What a real pilot requires

A real pilot requires authorized project records, agreed processing permission, and a named review owner. Useful documents include budget files, BOQs, contracts, actual spend records, invoices, purchase orders, schedules, progress reports, daily reports, site instructions, delay notes, material logs, equipment logs, ESG records, and correspondence. If evidence is missing, Constrovet should show the gap instead of guessing.

FAQ

Answers for readers evaluating the public synthetic demo.

Is the Constrovet demo based on real client data?

No. The public demo page uses a synthetic demonstration project only. No real client data, client result, testimonial, or audited project outcome is shown.

Why does the demo include cost and schedule figures?

The figures are synthetic examples used to show the type of executive output Constrovet can format when authorized project documents contain supporting evidence.

Can these synthetic figures be used as performance claims?

No. The synthetic figures are not customer outcomes, accuracy claims, savings claims, or proof of financial recovery.

What does a real Constrovet pilot require?

A real pilot requires authorized project documents such as budgets, BOQs, actual spend records, purchase orders, schedules, progress reports, ESG records, and permission to process them.

Does Constrovet replace professional audit or legal review?

No. Constrovet prepares evidence-backed project intelligence for review by project, finance, lender, consultant, audit, legal, and leadership teams.
