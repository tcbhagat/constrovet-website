ConstroVet focuses on a common project governance problem: leadership receives fragmented project documents, but needs a clear separation between baseline budget, leakage, overrun, schedule, and ESG evidence.

The product workflow is designed to extract quantified findings with citations, calculations, confidence, and missing-evidence checks so decisions can be reviewed by finance, project, and executive teams.
