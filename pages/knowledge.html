What makes a finding useful for executives?

A useful finding names the issue, shows the supporting evidence, preserves the financial category, and states what is missing. Constrovet is designed around that structure so the output remains defensible when reviewed by technical, finance, and leadership teams.

The knowledge pages avoid unsupported performance claims. They explain how the workflow should behave when evidence is available, and how uncertainty is handled when evidence is missing.
