Construction Project Due Diligence for Financiers

Give lenders, boards, and advisors cited project risk questions before decisions.

What It Shows

Constrovet organizes project documents into cited questions for lenders, investors, boards, and advisors. It shows budget, overrun, leakage, ESG, delay, recovery, and missing evidence.

What Financiers Need

Financiers need a clear view of budget, actual spend, leakage, overrun, ESG, delay, and recovery priorities. Constrovet structures those answers with citations.

Questions It Supports

  • Which findings are baseline budget and which are leakage or overrun?
  • Do Budget and Actual values support an Actual - Budget overrun calculation?
  • Are ESG metrics evidenced, missing, or mixed into unrelated financial claims?
  • Which 7/30/90-day actions should leadership review first?

When To Use It

Use it before funding, during monitoring, when delays trigger review, or when stakeholders need an evidence brief.

Evidence Standard

Every finding should include file, page or sheet, quoted span, amount or days, confidence, and missing-evidence notes.

Is Constrovet a replacement for lender diligence?

No. It prepares cited findings for review by financiers and their advisors.

Can the output support board reporting?

Yes. It organizes cited evidence, amounts, days, categories, and actions for stakeholder review.
