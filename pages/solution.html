Cost Leakage Review

Constrovet reads BOQs, budgets, actual spend, procurement records, progress reports, and site logs. It separates approved budget from leakage and overrun.

  • Classifies contract value, planned spend, BOQ value, and work done as baseline budget.
  • Flags penalties, wastage, rework, idle resources, delayed PO impact, and excess consumption when evidence supports it.
  • Calculates overrun as Actual - Budget when both values are available.

ESG Evidence Review

Constrovet extracts carbon, waste, energy, water, fuel, power, and emissions metrics when records contain them.

  • Keeps ESG metrics separate from financial leakage.
  • Links findings to source file, page or sheet, and quoted span.
  • Shows missing evidence instead of guessing metrics or causes.
