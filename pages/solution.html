Constrovet is a document-grounded construction project intelligence solution that helps teams convert budgets, BOQs, actual spend records, procurement logs, schedules, progress reports, and ESG records into cited cost leakage, overrun, schedule, carbon, and recovery findings. Cost Leakage Diagnostic Engine

Constrovet ingests project documents such as BOQs, budgets, actual spend, procurement records, progress reports, and site logs. The workflow separates baseline budget evidence from leakage and overrun signals.

  • Classifies standard contract value, planned spend, BOQ value, and work done as baseline budget.
  • Flags penalties, wastage, rework, idle resources, delayed PO impact, and excess consumption as leakage when evidence supports it.
  • Calculates overrun as Actual - Budget when both values are available.

ESG & Regulatory Evidence Review

The same evidence trail can extract ESG metrics such as carbon, waste, energy, water, fuel, diesel, electricity, and emissions when those metrics are present in the supplied records.

  • Keeps ESG metrics separate from financial leakage categories.
  • Links findings to source file, page or sheet, and quoted evidence.
  • Identifies missing evidence instead of guessing unsupported metrics or causes.

FAQ

What is the Constrovet solution?

Does the solution replace professional review?

No. It prepares evidence-backed outputs for project, finance, ESG, lender, legal, and executive review.
