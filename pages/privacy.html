Privacy Policy & Data Security

Last updated: May 20, 2026

1. Data Ingestion & Security

Constrovet provides project cost auditing services which require the ingestion of highly sensitive financial, schedule, procurement, and ESG data. Production deployments should use TLS, Google Cloud Secret Manager, private database access, and cloud-provider encryption controls for data in transit and at rest.

2. Use of Data for Audit Purposes

The data ingested, including material logs, labor hours, ESG metrics, and carbon tracking data, is used to calculate schedule slippage, cost leakage, and executive action plans. We do not sell, rent, or share proprietary project data with third parties.

3. ESG & Compliance Reporting

Reports generated regarding ESG compliance and potential regulatory review risks are kept confidential between Constrovet and the authorized user or financier.

4. Access & Retention

Users may request deletion of their project audit data. Upon contract termination or verified request, ingested data and generated leakage reports are removed from active systems according to the agreed engagement terms.
