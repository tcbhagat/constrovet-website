How Constrovet Works

From raw site data to actionable executive recovery plans — in four steps.

1

Ingest Project Data

Connect Constrovet to your ERP, site logs, and schedules. We ingest structured and unstructured data seamlessly to form the baseline of the audit.

2

Automated Audit & Analysis

Our engine compares actuals vs. baseline. It calculates cost slippage, converts schedule delays into monetary losses, and audits ESG metrics simultaneously.

3

Generate Bleeding Reports

Receive precise Cost Leakage and Cost Bleeding reports. Financiers can immediately see their return probability and exact areas of capital drain.

4

7 / 30 / 90-Day Executive Action Plan

We don't just highlight problems. Constrovet generates phased, actionable steps to recover schedules, mitigate NGT notices, and salvage project profitability — so decision-makers always know their next move.
