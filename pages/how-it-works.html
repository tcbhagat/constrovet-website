How Constrovet Works

From raw site records to cited executive recovery actions in four practical steps.

1

Ingest Project Evidence

Upload or connect available project files: budgets, BOQs, procurement logs, progress reports, schedules, material records, ESG logs, and related documents.

2

Classify Budget, Leakage & ESG

The workflow separates baseline budget values from leakage and overrun indicators, while ESG metrics remain in their own evidence category.

3

Generate Cited Findings

Findings include source file, page or sheet, quoted span, confidence, and calculations such as Actual - Budget where both values are available.

4

Produce 7/30/90-Day Action Plans

Leadership receives phased actions tied to the cited findings, helping teams decide what to review, recover, negotiate, or monitor next.

Designed for defensible decisions
