How Constrovet Works

How It Works

Constrovet gathers project documents, classifies the evidence, cites each finding, and turns findings into actions. It shows what is supported, what is missing, and what needs review.

Document-to-Action Workflow

Each step keeps evidence visible for review.

1

Gather Records

Use available budgets, BOQs, procurement logs, progress reports, schedules, material records, and ESG logs.

2

Classify Evidence

Separate baseline budget, leakage, overrun, delay, and ESG evidence.

3

Cite Findings

Keep source file, page or sheet, quoted span, confidence, and supported calculations.

4

Plan Actions

Turn cited findings into 7-day, 30-day, and 90-day recovery actions.

FAQ

What documents can Constrovet review?

Budgets, BOQs, actual spend, procurement files, schedules, progress reports, ESG logs, and site notes.

Does Constrovet guess missing values?

No. Missing evidence is stated clearly instead of creating unsupported amounts or causes.

What is the final output?

A cited executive report with supported findings, calculations, missing-evidence notes, and 7/30/90 actions.
