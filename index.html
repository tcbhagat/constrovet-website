What Constrovet Does

Constrovet is an AI-assisted construction project intelligence workflow for project owners, contractors, financiers, and consultants who need evidence-backed cost leakage, schedule delay, ESG carbon, and 7/30/90 recovery insights from available project documents. It separates baseline budget from leakage and overrun signals, keeps ESG metrics separate from financial claims, preserves missing-evidence notes, and presents reviewable executive outputs without claiming real client results or unsupported performance numbers.

FAQ

Conservative answers for project teams, owners, financiers, and consultants.

What is a construction cost leakage audit?

A construction cost leakage audit reviews project documents to separate baseline budget values from evidence-backed leakage signals such as penalties, wastage, rework, delay costs, idle resources, excess consumption, and calculated overruns.

Who is Constrovet built for?

Constrovet is built for project owners, SMB contractors, project managers, developers, financiers, and consultants who need a clearer view of construction project cost, schedule, ESG, and recovery evidence.

Does Constrovet replace a project audit or lender diligence process?

No. Constrovet is an AI-assisted project intelligence workflow that prepares evidence-backed findings for review by project, finance, lender, consultant, or leadership teams.

How does Constrovet handle schedule delay cost impact?

Constrovet reviews available schedule, progress, procurement, and cost evidence to identify schedule slippage and related cost impact when the supporting documents are present.

Can Constrovet support construction ESG and carbon audit work?

Constrovet can extract ESG and carbon-related metrics such as fuel, diesel, electricity, water, waste, energy, emissions, and carbon values when those values are present in project records.

Does the executive demo use synthetic data?

The public executive demo should be treated as a synthetic-data demonstration unless an engagement-specific dataset is clearly identified and authorized.

Founder Note

By Prof. Taran Bhagat. Constrovet is built around a practical construction management principle: executive decisions should be tied to project evidence, clear calculations, and visible missing-evidence checks.

The product is intentionally conservative. It is designed to support review, not to invent causes, amounts, client outcomes, or recovery claims that the documents do not support.
