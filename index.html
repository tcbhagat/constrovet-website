FAQ

Short answers for executive review.

What does Constrovet do?

It turns construction records into cited cost leakage, delay impact, ESG, and recovery findings.

Who is Constrovet for?

Project owners, contractors, project managers, developers, financiers, consultants, and executive review teams.

Does Constrovet replace audit or lender diligence?

No. It organizes evidence for review by project, finance, lender, consultant, legal, and leadership teams.

How are overruns handled?

When Budget and Actual both exist, overrun is calculated as Actual - Budget.

How are ESG metrics handled?

Fuel, power, water, waste, emissions, and carbon values stay as ESG metrics unless evidence supports a financial finding.

Is the demo real client data?

No. The public demo is synthetic and shows report format only.
